GPR for Estimating EM Duct Height

Implemented Gaussian Process Regression to predict evaporation duct height from sparsely sampled propagation data. Introduced an inverse-variance weighted approach (based off of Monte Carlo sampling) to tackle application of severe electronic sensor noise on new observations.

Characterizing EM Ducts with Neural Networks

Implemented multilayer perceptron using Tensorflow to quickly estimate evaporation duct height from sparsely sampled propagation data. In-depth discussion of model selection and limitations as a result of the necessarily small dataset.
