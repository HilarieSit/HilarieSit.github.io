Biography

I received my Ph.D. in Remote Sensing from Cornell University. My dissertation focuses on examining the application of machine learning methods for characterizing EM ducting within maritime settings. More broadly, my research interests lie at the intersection of computing and engineering.

Ph.D. Remote Sensing, Cornell University, 2021

M.S. Remote Sensing, Cornell University, 2020
